Pork chops With Nut Crust

312 kcal
30min

Products

  • 1) egg
    1 ½
  • 2) walnuts
    3 tbsp
  • 3) center-cut pork chops
    200 g
  • 4) parmesan cheese
    4 ½ tbsp
  • 5) black pepper
    2 pinch
  • 6) salt
    2 pinch
скачать список продуктов

Preparations

1

Preheat the oven to 400°F/200°C and line a baking dish with parchment paper. Shred the parmesan cheese and chop the walnuts. In a small bowl, mix together the shredded Parmesan cheese, walnuts, salt and black pepper.

2

In another small bowl or deeper plate, crack
the egg.

3

Lightly beat the egg.

4

Dip the pork chop in the egg.

5

Immediately after that, dip it in the walnut-parmesan mixture.

6

Place chops on the baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 20-30 minutes or until the crust becomes golden and crispy. Serve and enjoy!
